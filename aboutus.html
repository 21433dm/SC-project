ABOUT US

Welcome to the SC Project or to give it its full title The Student Client Project. This project came about because of our need for a group project, every time we need a group project we go through the same process of idea generation, idea rejection, idea generation, idea rejection and so on. Most of the time we’d struggle to agree on a single idea.

We then started to look on the internet for ideas for projects then we had a eureka moment… what if you could go on a website and find projects that other people need doing and do them as your college / university group project? The SC project was born.

Initially we are only going to be posting IT projects, networking, IT Repair, Web design / development and phone / tablet app based projects but there is scope to widen this when the website is established.

On the website you join as either a client or student, you can look for projects, add a project request and reply / respond to project requests. Initially the website will be limited in functionality but as it grows we intend to add more and more functions to make the process of finding or adding a project easier.

Click here to browse current projects or click here to create an account and post your own project for your organisation.

Should you have any questions or concerns please contact us and we’ll be happy to help.
